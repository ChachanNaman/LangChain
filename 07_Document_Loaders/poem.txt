The Sound of Cricket

Beneath the sun on fields so wide,
Where dreams and determination collide,
A bowler runs with focused eyes,
While the crowd erupts beneath blue skies.

The bat is raised, the moment near,
A hush replaces every cheer.
The ball is sent with pace and swing,
The willow answers — what a sting!

A cover drive through gaps so fine,
The ball races swiftly to the boundary line.
Four runs scored, the fans all shout,
Their joy and passion pouring out.

The spinner weaves a clever spell,
A turning ball that dips as well.
The batter swings but misses late,
The stumps are shattered by fate.

From village grounds to stadium light,
Cricket unites hearts day and night.
It teaches patience, skill, and grace,
And how to rise after every chase.

A final over, six to win,
The pressure mounts, the game grows thin.
One mighty swing, the ball takes flight,
Disappearing into the night.

Victory comes, the players smile,
Celebrating every mile.
For cricket is more than bat and ball—
It's a game that brings together all.
